Personal Carbon Calculator

We built this calculator to help ourselves learn how much carbon we put into the atmosphere just by existing. There are steps that each of us can do to reduce our impact, however, what does this personal responsibility look like when compared to global infrastructure and personal excess? We tried to generalize and gather accurate data but have made many assumptions about averages and material conditions. We know that this is not a perfect comparison but hope that it will help you as it did us to contextualize climate change and it's contributors.
